About this Initiative

Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) represents one of the aviation industry's most promising pathways to significantly reduce carbon emissions and work toward a more sustainable future for air travel. Virgin Atlantic, in partnership with other Virgin companies and external organizations, is leading the charge with this comprehensive trial program.

SAF is produced from sustainable feedstocks and has the potential to deliver significant lifecycle carbon reductions compared to conventional jet fuel. In this initiative, we're not only conducting technical trials with our aircraft but building the entire ecosystem needed to scale production and adoption of these revolutionary fuels.

Our Approach

The program takes a multi-faceted approach to advancing SAF:

  • Technical trials using SAF on commercial flights
  • Partnerships with fuel producers to increase supply
  • Policy advocacy to support investment in SAF technology
  • Customer education and engagement to build demand
  • Cross-industry collaboration to share learnings

The Impact

By 2030, our goal is to have SAF comprise at least 10% of our total fuel consumption, with continued growth beyond that. This initiative represents a critical step in our commitment to achieve net zero carbon emissions by 2050, while helping to establish the infrastructure and partnerships needed for the entire aviation industry to transition to a more sustainable future.
